Regional Significance

Li-Cycle's Arizona Spoke, located in Gilbert, Arizona, is a key part of their network serving the western United States. It processes battery manufacturing scrap and end-of-life batteries, converting them into black mass. This facility supports the growing battery ecosystem in the Southwest, providing a sustainable outlet for battery waste and contributing materials back into the supply chain via Li-Cycle's Hub facilities.
